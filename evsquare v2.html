01 / THE PROBLEM

Water hyacinths are
silently killing our waterways.

Listed among the world's most invasive aquatic plants, water hyacinths double in surface area every two weeks. They suffocate fish, choke irrigation systems, breed mosquitoes and destroy fragile freshwater ecosystems across India and beyond.

Manual removal is slow, dangerous and unsustainable. Chemical control poisons the lake. We built a third option — engineered, electric, autonomous.

🌊
200 million tonnes
of invasive aquatic biomass clogs Indian lakes every year.
⚠️
Doubles in 14 days
Unmanaged hyacinth blooms collapse fisheries and irrigation infrastructure.
🛶
Manual removal fails
Labour-intensive, unsafe, and unable to match the regrowth rate.
🤖
EV SQUARE's answer
An autonomous robotic vessel that harvests, shreds, and stores hyacinths — continuously, electrically, scalably.

03 / WORKFLOW

Four steps. One closed loop.

01
Collection
Boat autonomously navigates to dense hyacinth zones using GPS waypoints and obstacle-avoiding ultrasonic sensors.
🛰️
02
Conveyor
An inclined belt with paddle cleats scoops floating biomass from the water surface and transports it onto the deck.
⚙️
03
Shredding
Twin counter-rotating blades reduce the plant matter into uniform biomass chips, ready for downstream reuse.
🔪
04
Storage
Shredded biomass is collected in a sealed rear hopper. Operators dock and offload for composting, biogas or briquetting.

04 / TECHNOLOGY STACK

Built from first principles.

Every subsystem is engineered in-house — from the mechanical harvesting train to the embedded navigation stack. Modular by design, serviceable in the field.

📡
GPS Navigation
Multi-band GNSS receiver with waypoint following, geofencing and return-to-home failsafe.
±1.5 m accuracy
📶
Ultrasonic Sensing
360° obstacle detection ring detects shorelines, boats and floating debris in real time.
5 m range · 10 Hz
⚡
Electric Propulsion
Twin brushless thrusters give precise differential steering with zero emissions and minimal noise.
2 × 350 W BLDC
🔋
Lithium Battery Pack
Sealed marine-grade LiFePO₄ pack with integrated BMS, fast charging and thermal protection.
48 V · 2.4 kWh
☀️
Solar Trickle Charge
Optional flexible solar array extends operational runtime and supports remote deployments.
120 W peak
🌀
Harvesting Conveyor
Stainless-steel cleated belt operates at variable speed and clears in self-cleaning mode.
0.4 m/s · IP67
✂️
Shredding Mechanism
Twin counter-rotating hardened steel blades reduce biomass to uniform 20 mm chips.
220 rpm · 800 W
🧠
Edge AI Module
On-board vision model identifies hyacinth density, plans optimal path and avoids non-target plants.
Optional · Jetson Nano
📊
Telemetry & Dashboard
4G telemetry streams location, battery state, and harvest tonnage to a live web dashboard.
MQTT · 1 Hz
